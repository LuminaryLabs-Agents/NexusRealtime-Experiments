# LOD Policy

## LOD levels

```txt
LOD0: close prop with bevels and surface overlays
LOD1: mid-distance prop with simplified plank detail
LOD2: simple box silhouette
```

## Rules

- Preserve box silhouette first.
- Remove fine wood grain before removing bevel shape.
- Keep wet/worn state readable at mid distance.
- Track triangles and texture size.
