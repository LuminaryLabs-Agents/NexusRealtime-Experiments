# Status Board

This board tracks where ideas are in the Nexus Arcade pipeline.

## Raw

Ideas that are still loose, messy, or one-line concepts.

- `ideas/raw/example-game.md`

## Refined

Ideas with a clear pitch, loop, controls, and cabinet fit.

## Ready for Prototype

Ideas scoped tightly enough to build a small playable version.

## Playtest Candidates

Playable ideas that need feedback.

## Production Candidates

Ideas that have proven enough promise to expand.

## Shipped

Finished or released games.

## Merged

Ideas combined into another stronger idea.

## Retired

Ideas intentionally abandoned.

## Superseded

Ideas replaced by a newer or cleaner version.
